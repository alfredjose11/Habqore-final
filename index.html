About HABQORE

The Core of Corporate Intelligence

HABQORE is the strategic advisory and business intelligence division of HABIQON LIFE LLP, dedicated to helping organizations transform data into meaningful insights, informed decisions, and measurable business outcomes.

We integrate Financial Management, Business Intelligence, Performance Management, Governance, Operational Excellence, and Digital Transformation into a unified framework that enables leaders to make smarter, faster, and more confident decisions.

By combining expertise in finance, analytics, technology, and strategy, we deliver practical solutions that enhance visibility, improve performance, strengthen governance, and drive sustainable growth.

From executive dashboards and Power BI solutions to strategic planning, performance management, and business transformation initiatives, HABQORE empowers organizations to achieve operational excellence and long-term success.

HABQORE partners with organizations across diverse industries and sectors, delivering strategic intelligence, business advisory, and performance management solutions that empower leadership teams to drive operational excellence, strengthen governance, improve profitability, and achieve sustainable long-term growth. Regardless of industry or scale, our mission remains the same: transforming business information into strategic intelligence that creates measurable value.

Vision

To be the most trusted Corporate Intelligence and Business Performance Advisory partner, empowering organizations to achieve sustainable growth through data-driven leadership and strategic excellence.

Mission

To deliver integrated business intelligence, financial management, and performance improvement solutions that enable organizations to make informed decisions, optimize resources, strengthen governance, and create measurable value.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of ethics, transparency, and accountability in everything we do.
  • Intelligence: We transform data into actionable insights that support confident and informed decision-making.
  • Excellence: We are committed to delivering exceptional quality, measurable results, and continuous improvement.
  • Innovation: We embrace forward-thinking solutions and emerging technologies to drive business transformation.
  • Partnership: We build trusted, long-term relationships focused on our clients’ success and sustainable growth.
